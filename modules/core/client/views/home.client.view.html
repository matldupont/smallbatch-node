We all love food!

With a hint of Middle Eastern flare and a zest for modern fusion cuisine, Zaher's Small Batch perfectly suits Date Nights, Family Dinners and DIY Home Catering for when you're entertaining guests!

Further, we want to share our passion for food with you, and in the convenience of your own home... for a fraction of the cost of dining out!

ONEBox vs. BBQBox

Our ONEBox selection is our higher line of par-prepared take home meals!

Our BBQBox concept is just as cool, and a bit cheaper.

Our ONEBox selection is our higher line of par-prepared take home meals! All of these meals are prepared fresh to order and vacuum sealed for you to enjoy in the comfort of your own home! Instructions are included and all ONEBox courses are prepared as easily as dropping the vacuum sealed packages in boiling water for a few minutes, or grilling!

Our BBQBox concept is just as cool, and a bit cheaper. In every meal, you get: a side salad, grilling veggies, 8oz of meat and even the desserts go on the grill! No mess! Perfect for family dinners, camping or even entertaining large company for your outdoor occasion that you're celebrating!
